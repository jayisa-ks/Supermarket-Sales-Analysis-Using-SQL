# Supermarket-Sales-Analysis-Using-SQL
Analyzed 1,000 supermarket transactions using MySQL to identify sales trends, customer behavior, and product performance.

### Dataset
Records: 1,000
Tool: MySQL
Domain: Retail Sales

### SQL Concepts Used
. Aggregate Functions
. GROUP BY
. ORDER BY
. HAVING
. Subqueries
. CASE WHEN
. Date Functions

### Business Questions
. Total Revenue
. Revenue by Branch
. Revenue by Product Line
. Best Selling Product Line
. Member vs Normal Customer Revenue
. Payment Method Analysis
. Monthly Revenue Trend
. Weekend vs Weekday Revenue
. Customer Spending Analysis

### Key Insights
.Total revenue generated was ₹322,966.75.
.Branch C generated the highest revenue (₹110,568.71).
.Food & Beverages was the top revenue-generating product category.
.Members contributed more revenue than normal customers.
.E-wallet and Cash were the most preferred payment methods.
.Weekday sales accounted for nearly 69% of total revenue.
